Want to be safe on your planet explorations? Check out our line-up of weapons:

Weapon Name Basic Info Image

Lancer

 Fires blazing plasma shots at a rapid fire to obliterate foes!

Gravity Bomb

 Launches a small bomb out of its cannon and explodes on impact.
Even though the bomb seems small, it packs quite a punch!

Bouncer

 Launch a small bomb that bounces on the ground until it makes contact with an enemy,
then it will divide itself into several smaller bombs that explode on impact, obliterating anything in its path!

Plasma Coil

 Fires a plasma ball into the air and nearby foes will get zapped with plasma lightning!

Lava Gun

 Melt nearby foes with a constant stream of molten lava!

Sheepinator

 Transform anything (including both organic and inorganic stuff) into sheeps!

MiniRocket Tube

 Devastate enemies with this rocker launcher. Although the missiles look small, they pack quite a punch!

HoverBomb Gun

 Fires a spiky, explosive ball into the air and it will explode on input.
You can also control its trayectory. Use it well and nothing will stand a chance!

Shield Charger

 An absolute must for long explorations. Upon pressing the button, a force field will surround you,
protecting you from any hazard, from explosives, to mechanical jaws, extreme temperatures, you name it!

Zodiac

 Melt any foes in sight upon firing this device's zapping capabilities!
